📊 Bank Marketing Funnel Analysis
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a marketing funnel using the Bank Marketing dataset to understand how customers move from initial contact to final conversion.

The goal is to identify drop-off points, evaluate conversion rates, and provide actionable insights to improve marketing performance.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze customer conversion funnel
Calculate conversion rates at each stage
Identify drop-off points
Compare performance across customer segments and campaigns
Provide data-driven recommendations
📁 Dataset
Dataset Used: Bank Marketing Dataset (UCI Repository)
Total Records: 45,211
Features: 17
Target Variable: y (Term Deposit Subscription)

The dataset is based on direct marketing campaigns conducted via phone calls.

🔄 Funnel Definition
Stage	Description
Visitors	Total customers contacted
Leads	Customers with call duration > 100 seconds
Customers	Customers who subscribed (y = yes)
📊 Conversion Metrics
Visitor → Lead: 75.9%
Lead → Customer: 15.4%
Overall Conversion: 11.7%
📉 Drop-off Analysis
High drop observed between Leads → Customers
Majority of users engage but do not convert
📊 Segment Analysis
👥 Conversion by Job
Students → 28.7%
Retired → 22.8%
Unemployed → 15.5%
Blue-collar → 7.3% (lowest)
📅 Conversion by Month
March → 51.9%
December → 46.7%
September → 46.5%
May → 6.7% (lowest)
📢 Conversion by Previous Campaign Outcome
Success → 64.7%
Other → 16.7%
Failure → 12.6%
Unknown → 9.2%

🔍 Key Insights
High drop-off occurs after lead stage
Customer engagement is strong but conversion is low
Students and retired individuals have the highest conversion rates
Conversion is highly dependent on campaign timing
Previous campaign success is the strongest predictor of conversion
Longer call duration increases likelihood of conversion
💡 Recommendations
Focus on high-converting customer segments
Improve conversion strategies after initial engagement
Retarget customers from previous successful campaigns
Invest more in high-performing months
Reduce effort on low-performing segments
Enhance call quality to improve engagement
